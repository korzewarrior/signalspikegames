About

Signal Spike Games is a small studio focused on crafting tight, replay-worthy experiences. The goal: build games with zero fluff and 100% punch.

Signal Spike operates as a nimble, distributed crew. A tight senior core stays on every project, while seasoned specialists rotate in when their expertise can push the craft further.

Projects

Starboys working title

The first public release from Signal Spike—an arcade-scale competitive shooter being built in lock-step with a bespoke netcode stack. It's the proving ground for the tech that will power our bigger titles.

Star Tournament alpha build

An arena shooter with a fully playable core build that's already a blast. We're rebuilding from the ground up on brand-new netcode, adding fresh levels, and overhauling the art pipeline to get it ship-ready.
