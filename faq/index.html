FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who is Signal Spike Games?

Primarily a solo game studio that scales up when needed. We're building an asynchronous gamedev studio with a small, dedicated team working together on projects in a shared team environment. No unnecessary hierarchy, just autonomy and focused creative work.

What engine do you use?

Depends on the project. Starboys is built in Unity 6. Bloodrust uses Spectre Engine, a custom engine built in C. Null Online was built from scratch with a custom lightweight stack. We pick whatever fits.

Can I stream or make videos about your games?

Yes. You have full permission to stream, record, upload, and monetize content featuring any of our games. No need to ask. We'd love to see it.

How do I report a bug?

Join the Discord and post in the relevant channel, or email hello@signalspike.games. Include what happened, what you expected, and any screenshots if you can.

Are you hiring?

We work with collaborators on a project-by-project basis. If you do game art, music, sound design, or level design and want to work together on something, reach out at hello@signalspike.games with your portfolio.

Will your games come to consoles?

PC first, always. Console ports are something we'd like to do eventually but aren't promising yet. If it happens, we'll announce it.

Are your games free?

Depends on the game. Null Online is free. Starboys and Bloodrust will be paid games. We price things fairly and don't do microtransactions beyond the occasional joke cosmetic (see: Null Online's $3 gold pixel).

How often do you release games?

When they're done. We don't crunch and we don't rush. The roadmap on the homepage shows what's in progress and what's coming. Some of these are small experiments that take a day (like Null Online), others are bigger projects that take months or years.

Can I use your game assets, logos, or screenshots?

For press, content creation, fan art, or personal use: yes, go for it. For commercial use beyond that, email us first.

How do I get in touch?

Email hello@signalspike.games or join the Discord. We read everything.
