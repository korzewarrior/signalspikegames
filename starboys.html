Projects Starboys

Starboys

Alpha Cooperative Roguelike Shooter

Signal Spike's debut title: an arcade-scale cooperative top down roguelike shooter built in Unity with FishNetworking. Fast-paced, tactical, and endlessly replayable. It's our proving ground for game design principles that will drive our larger projects.
