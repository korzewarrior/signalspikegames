Game Jam

Narrative Adventure

Welcome to Chipperton! Chipperton thrives as one of the longest, most enduring communities in the midwest, being directly intertwined into the region’s history! The original Chippertonian families that founded this little haven were settlers with a penchant for exploration, a thirst for excitement, and a drive to solidarity.
