About

Signal Spike Games

Signal Spike Games is just me. I'm a solo dev building a studio from the ground up. I've loved games my whole life and eventually decided to stop thinking about making them and actually do it.

I do the design, code, and creative direction myself. When a project needs something I can't do — art, music, maps — I bring in contractors I trust. It's a small operation but it works.

I'm working on a bunch of different stuff. An arena shooter my kids can play, a dungeon crawler for people who grew up on Wizardry, a one-day MMO experiment, and a few more things further out. The homepage has the full roadmap.

I try to make games I'd actually want to play. That's really the whole philosophy. If something's fun, I keep building it. If it's not, I move on. The devlog has more detail on what I'm working on and how it's going.
