Star Tournament

Alpha Arena Shooter

An arena shooter with a fully playable core that's already a blast. Originally built in Unity 2019, we're rebuilding it in Unity 6 with FishNetworking, implementing fresh levels and overhauled art while perfecting the competitive multiplayer experience.
